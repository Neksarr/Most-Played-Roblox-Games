Most Played Roblox Games

Roblox is an online platform that allows millions of users to create, share, and play games made by the community. With its wide variety of experiences, from adventure to roleplay to competitive games, Roblox has become one of the most popular gaming platforms in the world.

Grow A Garden

Grow A Garden lets you plant, harvest, and design your dream virtual garden. Unlock rare plants, decorate with style, and relax in this peaceful Roblox world.

Blox Fruits

Blox Fruits is a sea adventure where players fight bosses, unlock devil fruit powers, and sail the ocean. With constant updates and a massive community, it’s one of Roblox’s most played games.

Steal A Brainrot

Steal A Brainrot is a hilarious chaos-filled Roblox experience. Players prank, troll, and challenge each other in unpredictable ways, making it a fan-favorite for laughs.

99 Nights in the Forest

Survive 99 Nights in the Forest by crafting, building, and escaping monsters. Every night gets harder, making survival an intense challenge that keeps players hooked.

Adopt Me

Adopt Me lets players raise pets, trade rare items, and build dream homes. With its massive trading system and creative freedom, it’s one of Roblox’s most iconic games.

Murder Mystery 2

A Roblox classic of mystery and deception where players take roles as Innocents, Sheriffs, or Murderers in a tense survival game.

Plants vs Brainrots

A parody defense game inspired by Plants vs. Zombies, where you battle waves of quirky brainrot zombies with silly strategies.

Jail Break

A cops-and-robbers style game where you can plan escapes, pull off heists, or enforce the law as a police officer.

Tower Of Hell

A randomly generated obby race where players climb obstacle towers without checkpoints speed, skill, and timing are everything.

Brookhaven

A popular roleplay game where you can buy houses, drive cars, and live out a virtual city life with friends.

Fish it

A relaxing fishing simulator where you explore waters, catch rare fish, and sell them to upgrade your gear.

Fisch

A multiplayer fishing game with unique fish species, trading, and progression perfect for collectors and adventurers.

Forsaken

A dark fantasy RPG filled with dangerous enemies, quests, and exploration in a mysterious, cursed world.
